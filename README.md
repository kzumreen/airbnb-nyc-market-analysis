# Airbnb Data Visualization & Market Analysis (NYC)
A comprehensive data analysis of the New York City Airbnb market, focusing on rental trends across boroughs, competitive pricing in Queens, and ROI calculations for property renovations.

📄 [View Full Analysis (PDF)](/Airbnb_Data_Visualization.pdf)

---

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Airbnb listing data to identify the most lucrative locations and property types in NYC. A specific case study is conducted for a property in Astoria, Queens, comparing it against competitors in Long Island City and Woodside to determine optimal pricing and renovation recovery timelines.

---

## Key Insights
- **Borough Comparison:** Manhattan leads the market with the highest number of rentals (10,969) and the highest average nightly price ($301.20).
- **Affordability:** The Bronx offers the most affordable rentals ($121.00 avg), while Queens and Staten Island maintain similar mid-tier pricing at $135.70 and $159.80 respectively.
- **Geospatial Trends:** Rental prices are directly correlated with proximity to major landmarks — neighborhoods near Central Park, Grand Central Terminal, and Wall Street command the highest rates.
- **Competitive Analysis (Queens 3B2B):** Long Island City represents a premium competitor ($420.50 avg), while Woodside offers consistent, budget-friendly competition ($360.27 avg across 11 listings).

## ROI Case Study: Astoria Host Analysis

| Metric | Before Renovation (2B1B) | After Renovation (3B2B) |

- **Break-even:** $15,000 ÷ $308.40 = **~49 rental days**
